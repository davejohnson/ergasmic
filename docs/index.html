How It Works

Three steps to structured training

Set Your FTP

Enter your Functional Threshold Power once. All workout intensities are based on this number.

Build a Workout

Add steady, ramp, and repeat steps using %FTP. Save it to your local workout library.

Connect & Ride

Pair your smart trainer over Bluetooth, hit start, and follow along with real-time feedback.
